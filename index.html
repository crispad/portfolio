Neighborbooks

To provide users with a simple and feature-rich solution for lending and borrowing books to/from fellow users that are located within their neighborhood.

React | Node | Express | Firebase | Google Maps API | Google Books API | Good Reads API

  • Collaborated remotely with a team of 4 Web Developers, to build a fully functional web application in 8 weeks.
  • Responsible for creating the landing page on the front end and connecting the Google Maps API.
  • Share books with neighbors, a system of loaning out books from your personal collection.
  • Keeps track of whom a user has lent out a book to.
  • Creates a digital library of a user's collection of books.
  • When books are lent out, keeps track of the borrowed date and due date.
  • Rating system for users to dissuade each other keeping books (AirBnB style)

About

I've learned different programming languages, along with different frameworks that have taught me how to create a front and back-end websites and applications.
I look forward to working in a company that will give me the opportunity to apply my programming skills. I'm ready to innovate, share my knowledge, and continue to learn.
